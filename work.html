Nationwide migration slowdown stalls Texas growth

Dallas Morning News | February 2026 | Web link | Google Drive link

For this article, I quickly contextualized how low migration rates have contributed to a slowdown in Texas’ population growth. While the slowdown in domestic movement to the state was shocking compared to previous years, Texas still retained its near No. 1 ranking among U.S. states. I resolved the apparent conflict between these two trends in the story by examining the wider context of decreased migration nationwide.

I reported, wrote, performed data analysis and made data visualizations for this article.

Mayor Brandon Johnson’s new speed cameras see big early revenue

Chicago Tribune | September 2025 | Web link | Google Drive link

Examining almost 850,000 rows of ticket data from Chicago’s Finance Department, I analyzed how much money the city’s new traffic speed cameras collected in the first half of 2025. Because of the data’s granularity, we were able to identify which specific sites hit drivers the hardest — exploring the intricate tension between traffic safety and revenue generation.

I performed data analysis, made data visualizations and interviewed residents for this story. I also co-interviewed quoted alderpeople and co-wrote this story with a City Hall reporter.
